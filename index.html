VectorID

On-device identification of Aedes aegypti, Anopheles gambiae, and the invasive Anopheles stephensi. Runs entirely on your phone — no connection needed after the first load.

Research demo — not yet field-ready. Field macro-F1 is ≈ 0.65, and the two Anopheles species are frequently confused — which is the core surveillance task. Uncertain results are flagged for expert review. There is no "not a mosquito" category, so any image is assigned to one of the three species.
